ATOMO CORPORATE CONTINUITY

Ensure the continuity of your work with a backup line.
The objective of guaranteeing the continuity of the work of our Customers represents a decisive element of our Offer.

For this reason, all Corporate solutions can include the Business Continuity service which intervenes automatically in the event of a breakdown of one or more lines.

Efficiency

• the service is automatically activated in the event of a breakdown;

• during normal operation all the connected lines are active.

Safety

• your company is more reachable and you do not miss business opportunities;

• reduce the risk of business interruptions;

• keep the same IP addresses even in the event of a main line failure.

How does it work

• the service is realized through a combination of two or more connections in failover or bonding mode in xDSL, FTTx or Radio technology.
